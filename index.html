Demo Application - For Educational Purposes Only!

This interface was created to allow advanced searching on legacy expeditions (2010-2020) as a project to study NODE/Express. One of its innovations includes subtotaling for each selection criterion, giving the user a preview of result totals beforehand. This helps in the determination of setting viable combinations thus avoiding zero sum queries.

NOTICE: The Art of Tech is not affiliated with NOAA nor is this an official NOAA site. The data was current at the time of collection; but information presented here is not being updated to reflect any potential changes, and links are not guaranteed to function as NOAA's site is updated.

Background

"NOAA Ocean Exploration supports research expeditions to explore previously unvisited areas of the ocean. OE provides partnership coordination, funding, staff, tools, and expertise needed to develop mission plans that deliver rigorous, systematic observations and documentation of biological, chemical, physical, geological, and archaeological aspects of the ocean. Follow OER-supported expeditions, including those on NOAA Ship Okeanos Explorer, while they are underway or trace the activities and discoveries of past expeditions by clicking on the expedition-specific links below. For each expedition, you'll find information ranging from mission plans and background essays to educational materials and mission logs, expedition daily updates, videos, and images."

The official site can be found at: https://oceanexplorer.noaa.gov.

Search Criteria

All selections joined together with boolean 'OR.' Select groups joined together with boolean 'AND.'
